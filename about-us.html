About Our Story

Transforming relationships through meaningful connection

Our Journey

The inspiration behind A Sex Journal for Couples came from our own personal experience. When we began documenting our intimate journey together, it completely revolutionized how we connected as partners. This practice unlocked meaningful dialogues we had never explored before, and to this day, maintaining our journal remains one of our most cherished relationship traditions.

From Idea to Reality

What began as a small passion project shared with close friends has grown into something much bigger. Our humble beginnings involved printing the first editions on regular paper and binding them by hand in our own living room. The enthusiastic response from our community motivated us to expand our vision.

We immersed ourselves in extensive research, consulting relationship experts, studying therapeutic approaches, and examining academic findings to develop a comprehensive format that truly resonated with couples.

Our Mission Today

Today, we're proud to be delivering journals and fostering meaningful conversations between couples across the globe. This milestone represents just the beginning of our mission to strengthen intimate relationships everywhere.

We're incredibly grateful that you've chosen to be part of our early community. Sharing this inaugural product with you brings us immense joy, and we eagerly anticipate hearing about your experience and insights.
